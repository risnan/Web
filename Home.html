Brand-X LOOK FOR A BEST RIDE


Brand-X was established in 1995 by veteran automobile engineer, Dr. Lawrence Perera with 20 employees and 1 factory. The objective was to engineer and build Sri Lanka's first truly home-grown automobile and the result was the launch of 'Privilege' which was completely designed and made in Sri Lanka. The first National Automobile was thus launched in 2001 winning an international patent for its unique design. The demand and acceptance for 'Privilege' amazed the country but the production constraints deprived Brand-x of meeting the escalating demand.

The company would develop a low-cost manufacturing and assembly operation that could produce high quality vehicles utilizing the resources and labor force of Sri Lanka. Today Brand-x Cars Limited remains the only national car manufacturer, assembling a full range of vehicles, from Compact Cars and Vans to Luxury SUV's, Buses and Prime Movers.

Brand-x ventured into overseas market by exporting vehicles into Pakistan, Bangladesh and Nepal in 2011. In 2012 Brand-x opened Sri Lanka's largest After Sales Centre to cater the demand of the ever expanding market share. Brand-x laid foundation for its 2nd manufacturing plant in Hambantota in 2014. Today Brand-x operates with 16 showrooms and 48 after sales service centres island wide. Today Brand-x has diversified into a group of companies with its subsidiaries Transmec Engineering (Asia's largest After-Sales workshop), Transmec International, Brand-x Construction and Euro Sports Auto Lanka (Franchisee of Lamborghini and many other luxury automobile brands) employing more than 1,000 employees, Having a portfolio of products to cater all groups of the communities. Brand-x plans to expand into the international market and exploit the opportunities of being a true National Pride!
